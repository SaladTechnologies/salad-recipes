Run popular LLMs with [Ollama](https://ollama.com/), a free and open-source tool for running large language models easily on consumer GPUs.
Works with a wide range of tools such as OpenClaw, OpenCode, Claude Code, Codex, and more. 
Ollama supports many popular models.

A few examples include:

[Qwen 3.5 family](https://ollama.com/library/qwen3.5)
- `qwen3.5:9b`
- `qwen3.5:4b`
- `qwen3.5:2b`
- `qwen3.5:0.8b`
- `qwen3-coder-next:latest`

[GLM models](https://ollama.com/library/glm-4.7-flash)
- `glm-4.7-flash:latest`
- `glm-4.7-flash:q4_K_M`

[OpenAI’s open-weight models](https://ollama.com/library/gpt-oss)
- `gpt-oss:20b`
- `gpt-oss:120b`

[Meta's Llama 3 family](https://ollama.com/library/llama3.2)
- `llama3.2:1b`
- `llama3.2:3b`

And many more from various sources, including Hugging Face and Ollama community contributions. You can also deploy your own custom models with Ollama.
[See the full list here.](https://ollama.com/search)
