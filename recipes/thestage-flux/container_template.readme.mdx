## How to Generate Images (cURL)

Save the generated image to a file:

<CodeBlock language="bash">{`curl -X POST https://${props.networking.dns}/v1/images/generations \\
  -H "Authorization: Bearer ${props.container.environmentVariables.AUTH_TOKEN}" \\
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \\
  -H "X-Model-Name: flux-1-schnell-s-bs1" \\
  -d '{
        "prompt": "sunset",
        "seed": 12,
        "aspect_ratio": "1:1",
        "guidance_scale": 0,
        "num_inference_steps": 4
      }' \\
  --output sunset.webp -D -`}</CodeBlock>
